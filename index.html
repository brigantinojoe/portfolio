About Me

Joe Brigantino is a California native from the Monterey Bay area with an athletic upbringing. During his time working after graduating San Diego State University, he realized the passion he had for efficiency.

Throughout the last few years, Joe learned how deeply he loved learning, and there's no better way to quench the thirst than learning to code. Little by little, he started automating parts of a large data integration project assigned by his employer, Pacific Coast Academy Charter School. He quickly became the Google Sheets Guru of the schools' accounting team, and continue to learn. Until he knew he neede more.. Then he stumbled upon the UCSD Coding Bootcamp and is destined to let his inner nerd show for all the world to see.
